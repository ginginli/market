What is supermarket simulator

Supermarket Simulator is a management simulation about building and operating a modern grocery store. Design your layout, stock shelves, set prices, serve customers at checkout, manage suppliers and finances, and expand from a small corner shop to a thriving market.

Multiplayer lets you co-manage the store with friends, coordinating stocking, checkout, and ordering tasks to run efficiently.

How to Play supermarket simulator

  1. Open and tidy the store; ensure aisles and checkout are clear.
  2. Order and receive stock; verify quantities and product freshness.
  3. Set competitive prices and place items on shelves by category.
  4. Serve customers efficiently; balance checkout speed and accuracy.
  5. Track cash flow, expenses, and margins; avoid stockouts and overstock.
  6. Reinvest profits to expand aisles, add new equipment, and unlock products.
  7. (Optional) Enable Multiplayer and assign roles for stocking, cashier, and ordering.

Tips and Tricks for supermarket simulator

  • Prioritize high-margin essentials; balance loss leaders.
  • Arrange shelves by category to reduce customer search time.
  • Adjust pricing dynamically based on stock levels and demand.
  • Restock during off-peak hours; keep checkout lanes clear.
  • In co-op, assign roles (stocking, cashier, ordering) for efficiency.
